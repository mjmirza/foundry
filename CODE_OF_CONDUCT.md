# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Foundry to be a welcoming project for everyone, regardless of experience, background, or identity. We pledge to make participation a harassment free experience for all.

## Our standards

Behavior that helps the community.

- Being kind and respectful to other people.
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback in good faith.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.

Behavior that is not acceptable.

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Publishing another person's private information without consent.
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would find inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and any public space where someone represents the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards and may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this code. To report a concern, contact the maintainer through the repository. Reports are handled with discretion.
